# awpapers

**awpapers** is a platform dedicated to showcasing and commissioning non-AI artwork. Our mission is to support human artists and provide a safe, secure space for art commissions.

## What We Offer

- **Non-AI Artwork Only:** All art featured and commissioned here is created by real artists—no AI-generated content.
- **Commission Platform:** Easily request custom artwork for social media, Discord profiles, websites, and more.
- **Safe & Secure:** We prioritize the safety and privacy of both artists and clients throughout the commission process.

## Why Open Source?

awpapers is proudly open source. By making our code and processes transparent, we empower our community to help shape the platform and ensure it remains true to its mission. Open source means:

- **Trust & Transparency:** Anyone can review our code to verify that we protect artists’ rights and client privacy.
- **Community-Driven:** Artists, clients, and developers can contribute ideas, features, and improvements, making awpapers better for everyone.
- **Accountability:** Open development keeps us honest and focused on supporting authentic, human-made art.
- **Security:** With many eyes on the code, vulnerabilities are found and fixed faster, keeping your data safe.

Whether you need a new avatar, banner, or any creative piece, awpapers connects you with talented artists ready to bring your vision to life—all while staying true to the values of authenticity, creativity, and openness.
